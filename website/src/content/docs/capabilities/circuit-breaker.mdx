`circuit-breaker` adds a circuit breaker wrapper to your Go service that stops cascading failures when external services degrade.

## What You Get

- Circuit breaker wrapper with open/half-open/closed states
- Configurable thresholds and timeouts

## When to Use

Any service calling external HTTP APIs, databases, or third-party services should use a circuit breaker. A slow external service will exhaust your connection pool and cascade failure to your callers — the circuit breaker trips open and fast-fails until the dependency recovers.

## Add to Your Service

```bash
archway new my-service --cap circuit-breaker
# or add to an existing service:
archway add circuit-breaker
```
